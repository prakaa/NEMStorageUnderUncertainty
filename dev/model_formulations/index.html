NEMStorageUnderUncertainty.build_storage_model — Function

\[\begin{aligned} \max_{t} \quad & \sum_{t=1}^T{\tau\lambda_t(p_t-q_t)}\\ \textrm{s.t.} \quad & u_t \in \{0,1\} \\ & p_t \geq 0 \\ & q_t \geq 0 \\ & p_t - \bar{p}\left(1-u_t\right) \leq 0\\ & q_t - \bar{p}u_t \leq 0\\ & \underline{e} \leq e_t \leq \bar{e} \\ & e_t-e_{t-1}- \left( q_t\eta_{charge}\tau\right)+\frac{p_t\tau}{\eta_{discharge}} = 0\\ & e_1 = e_0 \\ \end{aligned}\]
